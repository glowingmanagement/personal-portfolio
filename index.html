Head of Operations & App Development
April 2020 - Present

After starting with the Palace Drum Clinic to run small aspects of digital marketing, the pandemic very quickly put a halt to all of our live events. My role then became crucial to the growth of the business. As the business grew, my role now oversees the operational side of the business and also assist in the general business development to ensure we continue to grow.

Company Director
September 2020 - December 2021

Rubato Records was a project I set up throughout the pandemic. The aim was to educate and showcase upcoming artists throughout a very challenging period.

I ran weekly livestreams where I would interview independent artists to see what they were doing throughout lockdown. I also contacted industry professionals to run masterclass sessions. I received support from many professionals including, Danny O’Donoghue from The Script. I also worked with 2 international artists and we were able to offer recording packages for artists who couldn’t record from home. We would record instruments in our own home studios and create a product that the artists could release and still build their brand throughout this time.
